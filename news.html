News

14th Sept 2019 |
Estates defeated by Mccallum

Captain Jay of Mccallum won the match with 92 runs in 8.4 overs defeating the Estates with 3.6 overs left to spare. Estates played a good game achieving their target of 91 runs. But Mccallum chased them down using determination. Both captains did well in organizing and executing their plans. Mccallum now moves on to the finals.

14th Sept 2019 |
Marquis conquers Pearl

The Marquis team defeated Team Pearl in a long match. After winning the toss, Marquis chose to bat and Pearl decided to bowl. Marquis managed to obtain 103 runs with 8 wickets down in 12 overs. The Pearl managed to score 78 runs but lost all their wickets which resulted in their defeat. Pearl with David as their captain and Piyush as lead batsman were the favoured victors for the tournament. Captain Sarfraz using a well planned strategy brought glory to Marquis. Marquis now moves on to the finals.
