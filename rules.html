Rules

Know the rules
1. Each Team must have 14 players, any 10 can bat, bowl or field at any time.
2. 10 Wickets for each side.
3. Maximum 3 overs for two bowlers. Others can bowl maximum wo overs. For finals 4 overs for 2 bowlers, others can bowl max 2 overs.
4. All real cricket rules except LBW.
5. First 2 overs mandatory powerplay (3 fielder outside circle) and 1 over batting powerplay before 10th over.
6. First 3 overs powerplay for finals & 2 overs batting powerplay before 12th over.
7. 1.15 hrs for bowling team to finish inning, for finals 1.40 hrs.
8. All matches in semi-finals are of 12 overs and the finals is of 15 overs.
