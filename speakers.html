About Our Teams

Each team has been created with the sole purpose of making pickup and drop ups easy, thought has also been put into the creating the most balanced teams, inorder to create a competitive and challenging atmosphere for all the players.

Each team can have a maximum of 16 players out of which players who have classes can play first and can be subbed out later.
